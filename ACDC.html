OUR VISION
We envision a socially and economically empowered society free of poverty and gender disparities.
OUR CORE VALUES
We do everything: - To the glory of God; In total surrender and obedience to our Creator; For the
greater value in “WE” than “I”; With Integrity, Transparency and Accountability.
In recognition that Teamwork, Collaboration, Network and Partnership define
the relationships among ourselves, with organizations and the communities that we serve.
OUR LONGTERM STRATEGIES
Our long-term strategies include Mobilization, Training, Advocacy, Research, Networking, Relief and Support
OUR MISSION
To promote, advocate and foster socio-economic transformation of women, children,
youth and their communities towards poverty reduction through a sustainable
participatory approach.
OUR GOAL
ACDC’s overall goal is to create and increase access to socio-economic and educational
opportunities for marginalized children, youth, women and their communities.

WHO WE ARE

ACDC is registered in Kenya as a Not-For-Profit development facilitator whose overall goal is to create access to socio-economic opportunities towards empowering Women, Children and the Youth.ACDC works through a variety of strategies which includes mobilization of women and youth into self-governing community based groups (SHG/CBOs). Children, girl child protection and participation are advocated as a cross-cutting issue by partnering with the communities and socio-educational institutions such as families, schools, faith based and CBOs.
